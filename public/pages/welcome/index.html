Welcome to Narsheek

The effortless way to preview and manage your tabs without losing focus.

Getting Started

Ready to go? Just open the Narsheek side panel with the keyboard shortcut:

Press Command + Shift + X (on Mac)

or Ctrl + Shift + X (on Windows/Linux)

Pro Tip: Customize Your Shortcut

You can change this shortcut anytime. Go to your browser's extension management page:

Chrome/Edge/Brave: chrome://extensions/shortcuts

Firefox: Go to about:addons, click the ⚙️ icon, and select "Manage Extension Shortcuts."

Tired of a Thousand Open Tabs?

Modern browsing means juggling dozens of tabs, constantly breaking your concentration just to find the right one. Scanning tiny favicons and titles is a chore that scatters your attention and slows you down.

Your Focus, Uninterrupted.

Narsheek's core feature is **instant hover-to-preview**. Simply hover over a tab in the side panel to see its full content instantly. When you move your mouse away, you're right back where you started. No clicks, no context switching—just seamless flow.

You can also group related tabs and see which pages are already bookmarked, all within the same intuitive interface.
