Preview tabs instantly. Stay focused.

Tab.Show gives you full-page tab previews from a side panel. See before you switch and keep your place.

Getting started

Open the Tab.Show side panel right now with:

Mac: Command + Shift + X

Windows/Linux: Ctrl + Shift + X

Pro Tip: Customize your shortcut

chrome://extensions/shortcuts

See it work instantly

1. Hover over any tab name → full-page preview pops up.

2. Move your mouse away → you’re back on your original tab.

3. Click only when you’re ready to switch.

That’s it. You’ve just saved yourself from tab chaos.

Why it matters

Clear previews: no more guessing from tiny favicons.

Focus intact: your original tab stays put until you decide.

Handles scale: 30, 50, or 100 tabs - still manageable.

Zero bloat: one purpose only - previews.

Tips for power users

Pin the side panel for fast scanning during deep work.

Toggle with the shortcut as you switch tasks - it becomes second nature.

Preview before committing - no more misclicks, no wasted time.

Privacy first

Tab.Show does not collect your data. Built to cut clutter and protect your focus - nothing else.
